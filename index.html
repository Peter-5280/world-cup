Bonus points — group stage

Bonus points are earned per match in the group stage. They reward teams (and their owners) who dominate even when they don't go deep.

EventPer matchPoints
Win a group stage match× each win+2
Draw a group stage match× each draw+1
Clean sheet (0 goals conceded)× each match+2
Example calculation

Brazil wins all 3 group games, conceding 0 goals:
• Stage: 10 pts (advanced to R16)
• Wins bonus: 3 × 2 = 6 pts
• Clean sheet bonus: 3 × 2 = 6 pts
• Total: 22 pts just from the group stage alone.
