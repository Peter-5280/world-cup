Auto-draft (snake)

Teams are ranked by strength into tiers, then distributed using a snake draft: each player gets one team per tier, in alternating order, so everyone ends up with a balanced mix of strong and weaker teams. 32 teams are always distributed — if 32 doesn't divide evenly, the last few teams go to the first players.
